## Design Context

### Users
Busy UK dads aged 35-50 who train regularly but don't have time to obsess over nutrition. They open the app standing in the kitchen at 7am, meal prepping on a Sunday afternoon, or in Tesco deciding what to buy. They need answers fast — what to eat, how much, am I on track. They are not nutrition nerds; they are time-poor men who want to look and feel better without complexity.

### Brand Personality
**Crisp. Detailed. Precise.**

Forged Performance is a coaching brand that speaks like a knowledgeable mate, not a corporate wellness platform. The tone is direct, confident, no fluff. Every element earns its place. The app should feel like a precision instrument — a high-end watch face, not a cluttered dashboard.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Dark — used in kitchens, on phones, often early morning or late evening
- **Tone**: Premium and restrained. Think Porsche instrument cluster, not gaming overlay. Confidence through restraint, not through effects.
- **Anti-reference**: MyFitnessPal — cluttered, ad-infested, overwhelming, looks like it was designed by committee in 2014. Everything this app must NOT be.
- **Reference energy**: The shared mockup — deep navy, calorie ring, clean cards, branded header. But executed with more restraint and typographic precision than the current implementation.
- **Color direction**: Gold (#C9A84C) as the signature accent — used sparingly for emphasis. Teal (#00C9A7) for positive/progress states. Deep navy surfaces. No purple, no neon, no gradients on text.

### Design Principles
1. **Every pixel earns its place** — if it doesn't help the user eat right, remove it. No decorative filler.
2. **Glanceable over explorable** — the answer should be visible in under 2 seconds. No hunting.
3. **Precision, not density** — show detail without clutter. Generous spacing, tight data.
4. **One action per screen** — each view has a clear primary job. Don't overload.
5. **Built, not decorated** — quality comes from typography, spacing, and information hierarchy, not from shadows, glows, and effects stacked on top.
